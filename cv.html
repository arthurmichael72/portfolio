Selected Projects

Heavy Weight on Small Shoulders

Master's Thesis — Scrollytelling Data Story

An interactive, scroll-driven web experience communicating the impact of Seasonal Malaria Chemoprevention in West Mamprusi, Ghana. Combines watercolor illustration, D3.js data visualizations (animated bar charts, isotype grids, mortality charts), and culturally grounded comparisons to translate abstract health statistics for low-literacy audiences. Built on a Cultural-Visual-Participatory (CVP) framework drawing on ANS cognitive theory, Visual Rhetoric, Research through Design and Theory-Driven Research.
